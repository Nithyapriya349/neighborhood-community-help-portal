How It Works

A simple way to give and receive help

Our platform makes it easy to connect with your neighbors for everyday tasks and support.

1
Post a Request
Residents can easily create a help request, detailing what they need, from grocery runs to small home repairs.
2
Get Connected
Available helpers in the neighborhood can view the list of requests and accept a task they can assist with.
3
Complete & Confirm
Once the task is done, the helper marks it as complete, and the resident confirms, strengthening community bonds.
